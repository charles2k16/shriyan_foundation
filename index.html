Hosting Obah and Cheddar

Over the weekend, we had the pleasure of hosting Obah & Cheddar in Accra for an exciting city tour! We explored iconic spots like Independence Square, Kotoka International Airport, Accra Mall, and Achimota Mall.

  • This experience was more than just a tour — our goal was to inspire and encourage, especially those from rural communities, to keep growing and developing, no matter where they come from.
  • Today, it’s them… tomorrow, it could be you. Keep pushing. Keep building. Believe in yourself — your dream is valid and very possible.
